1 Browserling

It is hard to make a web page look consistent and useful in all the different browsers. Browserling makes it easy to test your shop in different browsers. Give it a try.

2 iphone and ipad compatiblity test

As per shopify founder 10% of all sale is done using mobile devices. It is important to have a shop that works on both iPhone and iPad.

ipadpeek.com is a cool site where you can check how your site will look in iPad.

Similarly iphone4simulator.com allows you to test your application in iPhone 4.
