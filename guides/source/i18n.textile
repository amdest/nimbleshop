h2.  Internationalization (i18n) and Localization

endprologue.

nimbleSHOP supports i18n. To use it first add following line to Gemfile.

<ruby>
gem 'rails-i18n'
</ruby>

Next open <tt>config/application.rb</tt> and uncomment following line

<ruby>
config.i18n.default_locale = :'pt-BR'
</ruby>

In the above case we are supporting the  Portuguese language for people of Brazil.
